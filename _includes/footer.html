About RiiConnect24

RiiConnect24 is WiiLink's service that lets you use the defunct WiiConnect24 service on your Wii once again; allowing you to use the Forecast, News, Everybody Votes, Check Mii Out and Nintendo Channel, along with sending mail to other Wiis.
